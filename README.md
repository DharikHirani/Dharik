This project reflects my commitment to leveraging technology for social good, using innovative solutions to tackle critical challenges. 

In the healthcare domain, I collaborated with ENT Head and Neck consultants and Radiologists to develop an MRI Brain Tumour Segmentation Model using nnUNet. The experience provided me with invaluable insights into the intersection of AI and medicine, further fuelling my desire to explore AI's potential in healthcare. 

Additionally, I have completed an intensive 12-week Data Bootcamp with JustIT, where I honed my skills in data analysis, visualisation, and cloud computing.
